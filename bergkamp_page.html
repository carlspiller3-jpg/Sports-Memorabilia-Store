Fast, Reliable & Signed For

UK Standard Delivery

  • Small items – via Royal Mail

At A1 Sporting Memorabilia, authenticity means everything - because it means everything to you.

Every signed product is issued with an official Certificate of Authenticity (COA), personally verified by our CEO, guaranteeing that every item has been genuinely hand-signed.

We believe our COAs are among the best in the world, providing you with complete peace of mind and protecting your investment for the future.

Each COA includes a number of security features to safeguard authenticity and prevent duplication or tampering.

Our Security Features

Premium printing and paper

Our Certificates are printed on high-quality, heavyweight textured paper, with sharp, intricate designs to prevent counterfeiting.

Integrated product or signing image

Where available, each Certificate features either a sample image of the signed product or a photo of the individual signing it, offering visual proof of authenticity.

Please note: Images are for illustration purposes only. Signature size and placement may vary.

Bespoke hologram seal

A custom A1 Sporting Memorabilia hologram, shimmering and animated when tilted, is applied to every Certificate for added security.

Unique online verification

Every item’s authentication code and product name are printed onto the Certificate. Enter the code on our website to view detailed product information and, where available, photographic proof of the signing.

Personally verified by our CEO

Every Certificate is personally verified and signed off by our CEO to guarantee the item’s authenticity.

FAQ's

How Do I Know Your Items Are Genuine?
At A1 Sporting Memorabilia, trust is at the heart of everything we do. We have been selling authentic signed sports memorabilia for over 25 years, earning the respect of both our customers and the sporting legends we work with. We conduct official signing sessions where players are fairly paid for each signature, building relationships based on trust, respect, and professionalism. Our customers choose us because we are open, transparent, and proudly stand behind everything we sell. We publish our names, our contact number, and our full address - we are real people, running a real business, with nothing to hide. Unlike anonymous sellers and online counterfeiters, we are proud to show you who we are and the legends we work with. See our latest signings and behind-the-scenes updates at: www.a1sportingmemorabilia.co.uk/news
Do You Offer a Certificate of Authenticity?
Yes - every signed item from A1 Sporting Memorabilia comes with an official Certificate of Authenticity (COA), featuring our bespoke hologram for added security. Regular products are issued with an A1 Sporting Memorabilia branded Certificate of Authenticity, personally authenticated and signed off by our CEO, Dan Jamieson. Where possible, each Certificate also includes a photo from the signing session, offering clear visual proof and extra peace of mind. However, a Certificate of Authenticity is only as trustworthy as the company behind it. With over 25 years of experience, transparent business practices, and direct, trusted relationships with the world’s greatest sports stars, A1 Sporting Memorabilia gives you total confidence in every purchase, backed by a name you can rely on.
Are Your Items Hand-Signed and Do You Offer Valuations or Buy Memorabilia?
Every item we sell is 100% genuinely hand-signed - we do not sell copies, prints, or facsimile signatures. We source all our memorabilia directly from private signings with the stars to guarantee authenticity. Please note, we do not offer valuations and do not purchase signed or unsigned memorabilia from the general public under any circumstances. All items we sell are exclusively obtained through official signing sessions to ensure the highest standards of quality and trust.
Can You Donate an Item to Our Charity?
A1 Sporting Memorabilia supports a small number of chosen charities, including Julia’s House – Dorset & Wiltshire Children’s Hospices. While we focus our efforts on these key partners, we may be able to help with your charity event. Please contact [email protected] with details of your request, and our team will be happy to advise.
