# Code of Conduct

## 1. Professionalism & Ethics
*   **Authenticity Integrity**: The cornerstone of our business is trust. We have a **zero-tolerance** policy for knowingly selling fakes or misrepresenting the condition/provenance of an item. All doubts about an item's authenticity must be raised immediately.
*   **Customer Service**: Trent customers with respect, honesty, and patience. Responses to inquiries should be timely and professional.

## 3. Representation
*   When representing the brand (online, at events, or in communication), individuals must maintain a professional image. 
*   Avoid engaging in public disputes or negative commentary about competitors or customers on social media.

## 4. Conflict of Interest
*   Contractors should declare any other business interests that might conflict with this role (e.g., running a direct competitor store using our supplier data).

## 5. Dress Code
*   If attending video calls with clients or physical events, appearance should be smart-casual and presentable.
