# IT & Data Security Policy

## 1. Overview
This policy outlines the acceptable use of IT equipment and data security measures for [Business Name]. It is designed to protect customer data (GDPR compliance) and the intellectual property of the business.

## 2. Equipment Security
*   **Devices**: All laptops/computers used for business must be password/PIN protected.
*   **Software Updates**: Operating systems and antivirus software must be kept up to date.
*   **Screen Locking**: Devices should be locked when left unattended, even in a home environment if others are present.

## 3. Data Protection
*   **Customer Data**: Personal data (names, addresses) must ONLY be stored on authorized secure platforms (e.g., the website CMS, secure cloud storage). It must never be saved to local desktops or unsecured USB drives.
*   **Passwords**: Use strong, unique passwords for all business accounts. Do not share passwords via plain text email/messaging. Use a password manager if provided.
*   **Phishing**: Be vigilant against email scams. Verify sources before clicking links or downloading attachments.

## 4. Acceptable Use
*   **Business Email**: The business email address is for professional use only.
*   **Social Media**: When posting on behalf of the business, adhere to the Code of Conduct. Do not share sensitive business info (turnover, supplier details) on personal channels.

## 5. Breach Reporting
Any suspected data breach (e.g., lost laptop, accidental email to wrong recipient) must be reported to the Business Owner immediately to comply with ICO reporting timeframes (72 hours).
