# Accessibility Policy

## 1. Statement of Commitment
[Business Name] is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

## 2. Web Accessibility
We aim to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA.
*   **Images**: All product images of memorabilia will include descriptive Alt Text (e.g., "Signed Lionel Messi Framed Shirt").
*   **Navigation**: The website will be keyboard navigable.
*   **Contrast**: We ensure sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds (e.g., avoiding light grey text on white).

## 3. Communication
*   We provide multiple methods of contact (email, text, phone) to accommodate different communication needs.
*   Documents/invoices can be provided in large print upon request.

## 4. Physical Events (If applicable)
*   When booking stalls for fairs or events, valid attempts will be made to ensure the venue is wheelchair accessible.

## 5. Feedback
We welcome feedback on the accessibility of our services. Please contact us at [Email] if you encounter barriers.
