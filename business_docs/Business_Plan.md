# 1. Overview

## Business Overview
The Sports Memorabilia Store is a premium sports memorabilia retailer based in England, United Kingdom, specialising in verified, athlete-signed memorabilia. The business focuses on delivering authenticated, high-quality autographed items that combine emotional significance with long-term collectible value. Our customers include sports fans, collectors, gift buyers, and investors who value authenticity, provenance, and presentation.

The Sports Memorabilia Store was created in response to widespread distrust in the memorabilia market, where counterfeit items, weak authentication standards, and poor customer experiences have become commonplace. Our objective is to establish a clear, trusted alternative by offering proof-based authenticity, transparent sourcing, and a premium end-to-end buying experience.

The business operates primarily through a direct-to-consumer online model, allowing full control over branding, verification standards, pricing, and customer relationships. While football is the initial focus due to its global reach and demand, the platform is designed to expand into additional sports including boxing, rugby, cricket, motorsport, and American sports.

## Mission Statement
Our mission is to become the most trusted destination for authentic signed sports memorabilia in the United Kingdom and internationally. We aim to preserve sporting history by offering verified, athlete-signed collectibles that fans can purchase with confidence and pride, while delivering an unforgettable gifting and ownership experience.

## Executive Summary (The Problem & Solution)
**The Problem:**
The global sports memorabilia market is severely undermined by counterfeit goods, unregulated sellers, and inconsistent authentication practices. Consumers are frequently exposed to forged signatures, misleading Certificates of Authenticity, and unverifiable claims regarding provenance. This problem is especially prevalent on secondary marketplaces and informal resale platforms. For many buyers, particularly gift purchasers, the consequences are significant—turning what should have been a proud moment into a costly disappointment.

**The Solution: A Premium Gifting Brand**
The Sports Memorabilia Store addresses this by positioning itself not just as a memorabilia seller, but as a **Premium Gifting Brand**. We recognize that the true product is not the "signed item", but the *sentiment* and the *moment* of gifting. 

**Brand Doctrine:**
"We create premium sports gifting experiences people are proud to give."
We do not sell "products". We sell identity, emotion, validation, and nostalgia — wrapped in a frame, proofed, and delivered fast.

We differentiate through:
1.  **"Modern Heritage" Luxury**: Apple-style unboxing and museum-grade framing that beats 90% of the market.
2.  **Tech-Enabled Authenticity**: A custom-built platform (React 19 + Supabase) that generates immutable Digital COAs, solving the trust deficit.
3.  **The "Gifting" Funnel**: A user experience built around occasions (Birthdays, Christmas), not just inventory lists.

## Brand Identity: "Modern Heritage"
*   **Core Concept**: A luxury gifting brand disguised as a memorabilia store.
*   **Palette**: Warm Ivory (#F9F7F3), Charcoal Graphite (#2E2E2E), and Champagne Gold (#C6A664).
*   **Tone**: Calm, Assured, Provenance-led. "Authentic Moments. Signed Forever."

# 2. Market Research

## Industry Overview & Market Opportunity
The global sports memorabilia market was valued at approximately $26 billion in 2021 and is projected to grow significantly over the next decade, with estimates reaching over $200 billion by 2032. Growth is driven by increased digital commerce, global fan engagement, and rising interest in collectibles as alternative assets.

Football memorabilia represents one of the strongest segments within this market due to the sport’s global reach and emotional following. Demand for authenticated, framed, and proof-based memorabilia continues to rise. Despite this, the market remains fragmented, with no clear leader in premium, proof-driven memorabilia retail. This presents a significant opportunity for The Sports Memorabilia Store to establish itself as a trusted brand.

## Target Customers
The business serves several key customer groups:
1.  **Sports Fans**: Seeking meaningful items connected to their favourite players or teams.
2.  **Gift Buyers**: Purchasing for birthdays, Christmas, holidays, and milestone occasions. They value packaging and assurance.
3.  **Collectors**: Looking for verified memorabilia with long-term value and provenance.
4.  **Corporate Clients**: Sourcing premium gifts for clients, partners, or stiff.

# 3. Products & Services

## Core Offerings
The Sports Memorabilia Store offers a curated selection of athlete-signed sports memorabilia:
*   **Framed and Unframed Signed Football Shirts**: The flagship product.
*   **Signed Equipment**: Football boots, balls, and goalkeeping gloves.

## Services & Funnels
Our revenue is driven by three specific sales funnels:

1.  **Direct Product Drop**: High-scarcity releases (e.g., "Signed Messi Shirt - Limited to 50") drive immediate traffic via Email/SMS.
2.  **Occasion Gifting Flow**: A "Gift Finder" quiz on the homepage asking "Who is this for?" -> Curated recommendations (reducing paralysis).
3.  **Corporate Gifting**: A dedicated B2B service for client appreciation (Bulk orders, custom plaques).

## Future Expansion (Roadmap)
*   **Appraisal Services**: Professional valuation (Planned Year 2).
*   **Consignment**: Selling on behalf of collectors.
*   **Members Club**: Exclusive early access to private signings.

## Business Models
The business operates primarily as a **Direct-to-Consumer (DTC)** online retailer.
*   **Revenue Source**: generated through the sale of highly-margin framed products.
*   **Occasion-Led Growth**: Rather than relying on recurring subscriptions, the business uses occasion-led purchasing behaviour (Father's Day, Xmas) as its primary driver.
*   **Gifting Plans**: Optional pre-paid gifting plans may be offered to spread costs for high-value items, structured as fixed-term agreements.

# 6. Operations & Technology
## 6.1 The "Tech-Enabled" Platform
Unlike competitors using generic templates, we run valid **Proprietary Technology**:
*   **Stack**: Custom **React 19** Frontend with **TailwindCSS v4** for pixel-perfect luxury UX.
*   **Backend**: **Supabase** database handling "Digital Assets" (Inventory, COA generation).
*   **Asset**: The "Digital COA" system is a custom intellectual property that adds significant valuation to the company.

## 6.2 The "6-Zone" Operational Workflow (Stock Room Architecture)
To ensure scalability and "24-Hour Dispatch" speed, the workspace is organised into 6 distinct zones:
1.  **Zone 1 - Intake & Sorting**: Arrival of raw items & immediate "Triage" (Logged -> Photographed -> Barcoded).
2.  **Zone 2 - Proof Library**: Digitisation of signing evidence. File Standard: `PLAYER_ITEMTYPE_DATE_VARIANT`.
3.  **Zone 3 - Frame Prep**: Clean area. Mount board, acrylic, air blowers. No clutter permitted.
4.  **Zone 4 - Finished Stock**: Ready-to-go inventory. Visible fronts, organised by Athlete.
5.  **Zone 5 - Packaging**: The "Speed Station". Boxes, tissue, branded stickers, dispatch labels.
6.  **Zone 6 - Returns/Repairs**: Quarantine area for resolution.

## 6.3 Packaging & Unboxing (The "Apple-Style" Standard)
We treat packaging as a marketing channel. Every item undergoes a **10-step Quality Control (QC)** check:
1.  **Clean Station** (Wiped tables).
2.  **Frame Wipe-Down** (No fingerprints).
3.  **COA Placement** (Bottom-right corner).
4.  **Tissue Wrap** (Premium fold).
5.  **Branded Seal** (Gold seal application).
6.  **Padding Protection** (Corner guards).
7.  **Box Fitting** (Snug fit).
8.  **Label Print** (Dispatch check).
9.  **Tracking Upload**.
10. **Dispatch Confirmation**.

## 6.4 Supplier Tiers (Risk Management)
*   **Tier 1 (70% Stock)**: Primary, bulk-friendly supplier.
*   **Tier 2 (20% Stock)**: Secondary, used for overflow and price-checking.
*   **Tier 3 (10% Stock)**: Opportunistic, used for rare one-off items.
*   **Authenticity**: Mitigated by strict chain of custody and video proof.
*   **Transit Damage**: Mitigated by specialized packaging and full insurance.
