## 2. THE BUSINESS MODEL: SALES & SERVICES
We generate revenue through high-margin product sales and low-risk service fees.

### Channel A: The Hero Drop (Product Sales)
*   **Mechanism:** Periodic releases of "In-House" signed stock (e.g., The Liverpool Collection).
*   **Product:** Premium Framed Shirts, Boots, Gloves.
*   **Avg Order Value (AOV):** £300.

### Channel B: Send-In Services (Service Revenue)
*   **Mechanism:** Customers send *their* own items to be signed at our private events.
*   **Price:** £99 - £149 per item (Sign + COA + Return Ship).
*   **Margin:** Extremely High (No stock risk, purely time/access).

### Channel C: Framing Services
*   **Mechanism:** "Upgrade your shirt". Converting customer-owned raw shirts into premium wall art.
*   **Price:** £150+ per frame.

**Phase 2: The "Vault Drop" Event**

*   **Execution:** We sign 100+ items in one day, fulfilling all pre-orders instantly.

## 5. RISK MANAGEMENT
| Risk | Mitigation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Inventory Risk** | We focus heavily on **Pre-Orders** and **Send-Ins** to fund the signing fee before spending capital. |
| **Counterfeits** | Tech Stack (Vikas) builds the NFC Digital Chain of Custody. Every item is tracked. |
| **Market Rates** | If athlete fees rise, we adjust RRP. Premium positioning allows price elasticity. |
